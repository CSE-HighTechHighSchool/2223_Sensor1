Step 1

Slide the pink wristband onto your wrist and make sure that the black box is facing up and does not slide.

Step 2

Remove the adhesive on the three electrodes and prepare to place them on your arm

Step 3

Place each of the electrodes onto your wrist, as indicated by the picture.

Step 4

Go to the TremorTrackr website. (on a mobile device or computer, although a computer is recommended for larger graphics)

Step 5

If you haven't created a TremorTrackr account, register with an email address and newly created password.

Step 6

Sign into the TremorTrackr website with the email and password that you have been using to access your account and data.

Step 7

When you are ready to start tracking your tremors, click the “Start Collecting Data” button on the screen.

Step 8

When you are finished, click the "Stop Collecting Data” button. Now view graphs of your collected data.

Step 9

The wristband can now be removed from your forearm and you can log out of the website. Your data will saved.

Step 10

Consult with a medical professional to analyze the TremorTrackr data. They will decide upon a treatment to reduce the severity.
