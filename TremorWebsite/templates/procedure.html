Step 1

Slide the pink wristband onto your wrist and make sure that the black box is facing up and does not slide.

Step 2

Remove the adhesive on the three electrodes and prepare to place them on your arm

Step 3

Place each of the electrodes onto your wrist, as indicated by the picture.

Step 4

Go to the TremorTracker website. (on a mobile device or computer, although a computer is recommended for larger graphics)

Step 5

If you haven't created a TremorTracker account, register with an email address and newly created password.

Step 6

Sign into the TremorTracker website with the email and password that you have been using to access your account and data.

Step 7

When you are ready to start tracking your tremors, click the “Start Collection” button on the screen.

Step 8

Ensure that the device is securely on your wrist, and the electrodes are always in place. This is essential to track your tremors.

Step 9

When you are finished, click the “End Collection” button. Now view graphs of your collected data.

Step 10

The wristband can now be removed from your forearm and you can log out of the website. Your data will saved.

Step 11

Consult with a medical professional to analyze the TremorTracker data. They will decide upon a treatment to reduce the severity.
