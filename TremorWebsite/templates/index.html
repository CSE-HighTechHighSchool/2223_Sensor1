What Is TremorTracker?

TremorTracker is a state-of-the-art wearable sensor device for Parkinson's patients. The device is worn on the arm, and by using precise accelerometry and electromyography sensors, your tremor is quantified and classified throughout the day. Helps to track the effectiveness of your medication and treatments.
